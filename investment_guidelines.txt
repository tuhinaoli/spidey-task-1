SUBJECT: Capital Allocation Audit and Project Feasibility Review

=========================================
1. Executive Mandate & Project Scope
=========================================
We are auditing three competing regional capital expansion projects (Project Alpha, Project Beta, and Project Gamma) to select the single optimal investment for the upcoming fiscal year. Our objective is to maximize net present value (NPV) and internal rate of return (IRR) while strictly complying with corporate risk, budget, and hurdle rate policies.

=========================================
2. Core Financial Constraints
=========================================
The capital allocation decision is governed by three strict compliance caps:
- Capital Budget Cap: The total initial capital expenditure (Capex) for the selected project cannot exceed $390,000. Due to liquidity limits, we can fund at most one project.
- Hurdle Rate Cap: The Internal Rate of Return (IRR) of the project must be greater than or equal to 15.00%. Any project below this rate is automatically rejected.
- Net Present Value (NPV) Cap: The project must deliver a positive Net Present Value (NPV) of at least $50,000.

=========================================
3. Mathematical Formulas
=========================================
All calculations must be performed using the following exact formulas:
- Net Present Value (NPV):
    NPV = -Capex + Sum_over_t [ Cash_Flow_t / (1 + r)^t ]
  Where:
    - Capex is the initial capital expenditure at Year 0.
    - Cash_Flow_t is the net cash flow in Year t (t = 1 to 5).
    - r is the risk-adjusted discount rate.
    - t is the year (1, 2, 3, 4, 5).

- Internal Rate of Return (IRR):
    Solve for IRR where NPV = 0:
    Capex = Sum_over_t [ Cash_Flow_t / (1 + IRR)^t ]
    (Express IRR as a percentage rounded to two decimal places).

=========================================
4. Risk-Adjusted Discount Rates
=========================================
The base discount rate is determined by the regional economic indicator. However, due to political and market volatility, we apply a risk-adjusted modifier:
- PESTEL Risk Modifier: If a project's region has a PESTEL risk score greater than or equal to 7 (or classification "High" in regional_economic_indicators.csv), the discount rate must be increased by a risk premium of 2.00% (0.02) over the base discount rate.
- For all other regions (Low or Medium risk), the base discount rate is used directly without adjustment.

=========================================
5. Corporate Policy Overrides
=========================================
Two critical overrides have been approved by the board for this planning cycle and must override static database values:
- Green Initiative Subsidy (Capex Override): To promote sustainability, Project Beta (located in eu-west) is eligible for a 15.00% green subsidy. This subsidy directly reduces the initial capital expenditure (Capex) listed in the CSV by 15.00% for all NPV and IRR calculations.
- Franchise Royalty Fee (Cash Flow Override): For Project Alpha (located in us-east), the Year 4 cash flow shown in the projected cash flow chart represents gross revenue. To determine net cash flow for NPV and IRR calculations, this Year 4 gross cash flow must be reduced by a 20.00% franchise royalty fee.
